Ensembl Image Reuse

Definition

Image reuse is the extraction of images from the Ensembl resource for the use in other media e.g. as a figure in a manuscript. Media can include presentations, journal manuscripts, academic manuscripts, newspapers, websites or books. No distinction is made between online or printed media. Ensembl resource refers to the Ensembl website, tools and data produced by Ensembl.

Policy

Images generated or captured, by screenshot, from the Ensembl website may be used in other materials. This includes any service which may receive funds from the redistribution of those images. All use should be accompanied with a citation back to Ensembl.
